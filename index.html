About The Firm

TJAJO & Partners is one of the well-respected corporate law firms in Indonesia. We have a long-standing client relationships with multinational and leading corporations in various industry sectors, financial institutions, and relevant government entities. We earn the reputation due to our commitment to outstanding service and professional excellence.

We take a careful understanding on the culture and the legal business environment in Indonesia and tailor each professional service to accomplish your business challenges. We continuously enrich the knowledge and evolve in the anticipation of the rapid legal environment.We practice in a true commercial approach that promises better value to our clients.

We have worked and represented our clients in various cross-border deals and complex transactions, in which some of those have received and/or being nominated for most notable deals by various prominent legal directories.

We have built longstanding relationships with the relevant government institutions in the industry to provide comprehensive service on a national and international scale.
